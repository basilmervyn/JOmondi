AFRICA FOR SDGs

Africa for SDGs is an organization that deals with the training of ESD and SDGs in the community our mission is to be the leading nation in transforming the world through competency-based learning, our vision is to develop the present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs, our motto is ESD training the world.

Aims of ESD is to empower and equip current and future generations to meet their needs using a balanced and integrated approach to economy, social and environmental dimensions. As KU-UNESCO member and an SDG fellow we spearheaded a wall art mural in KU to help in awareness of the SDGs within the university we also had an outreach with the KU primary scouts.
